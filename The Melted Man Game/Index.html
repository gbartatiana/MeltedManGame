The Melted Man Game

When the weather does not lend itself to an outdoor game, we must find an occupation for our children.

The Melted man Game, which is a derivative of the hangmanâ€™s game, is ideal to occupy children indoors.

Playful and fun, this electronic version of the game is easy to use.

Basically, this game is played with two people, using a paper and a pencil.

In this electronic version, the second person is replaced by the computer and, paper and pencil are replaced by the computer screen and the keyboard.

The goal of the game is to find a word by guessing the letters that compose it before the man is more than a simple pile of water on the ground.

How to play

  • When you start a game, a word is randomly selected by the computer from a list.
  • The number of letters the word contains is displayed.
  • Below this information appears the input field for the letter you wish to search.
  • After entering a letter, you must click on the Search button to the right of the input area.
  • The following section displays strokes that may be replaced by the letters of the word when guessed.
  • Finally, the letters you used to try to find the mystery word are displayed.
  • The game ends when the mystery word has been found or, that the man is completely melted.
  • You have a maximum of 8 errors before the man disappears from the screen.
